Don't know what event to choose? Let the game devise a random vote with 5 options by typing !buy event torytalkervote or !buy event hodlbotvote

Find the "eventname" of the item you want, and type in the Twitch chat: "!buy (eventname) "

ex: "!buy raid 1500" would send a low tier raid to me. Watch your karma! Having lower karma lowers the ammount of coins you gain over time; the lowest karma you can have is 20, which would give you .20 of your usual coin gain. Higher karma will give you more coins though! 150 karma would be 150% more coins.

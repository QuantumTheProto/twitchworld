TwitchToolkit(Danny's stream version)

TwitchToolkit is a mod written by Twitch.tv/hodlhodl that integrates storytelling decisions into chat votes, awards viewers coins for watching, and those coins can be spent on items/events in game.

I am currently using Nesi's Rimvali mod, along with Reich Armory and Soviet Armory. Below you can find the list of purchaseable in game items. Click on the header of a row to sort by that variable (Note: Price sorting doesn't work at the moment.) When purchased, they will be sent to the colony using a drop pod, and I will have full control of the item from there.

Use !bal to check your coin balance!

Find the "itemname" of the item you want, and type in the Twitch chat: "!buy (itemname) (ammount)"

ex: "!buy kidney 100" would send 100 kidneys to me. Minimum purchase worth 10 coins.

Click on "Events" in the top bar to see the list of purchaseable events!
